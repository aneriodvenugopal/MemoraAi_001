## Template 1: new_lead_intro
**When to use:** Agent adds a new lead contact → Lead receives first WhatsApp message
**Category:** MARKETING
**Language:** English

**Header:** TEXT
```
Welcome to {{1}}! 🏠
```
*Variable {{1}} = Agent/Company Name*

**Body:**
```
Hello {{1}},

Thank you for your interest in real estate properties! I'm {{2}}, your dedicated property consultant from {{3}}.

I have some excellent property options in {{4}} that may interest you. I'd love to understand your requirements better and help you find the perfect property.

Feel free to reply to this message or call me directly for a quick discussion.
```

**Variables:**
- {{1}} = Lead Name (e.g., "Ramesh")
- {{2}} = Agent Name (e.g., "Sai Kumar")
- {{3}} = Company Name (e.g., "RealApex")
- {{4}} = Location (e.g., "Vijayawada")

**Footer:**
```
Powered by AgentApex | Reply STOP to unsubscribe
```

**Buttons:**
1. QUICK_REPLY: "View Properties"
2. QUICK_REPLY: "Call Me Back"

## How to Submit for Approval

### Meta WhatsApp Manager:
1. Go to: https://business.facebook.com/wa/manage/message-templates/
2. Select WABA: "Eloniot Software Solutions"
3. Click "Create Template"
4. For each template:
   - Name: (template name above - lowercase, underscores)
   - Category: MARKETING
   - Language: English
   - Fill Header, Body, Footer, Buttons as shown above
5. Click "Submit"

### Approval Time:
- Usually 5 minutes to 24 hours
- Marketing templates may take longer
- Keep text professional, no spam language
- Variables must have sample values during submission
